Blog

Sri Lanka is so much more than it's famous sites, magnificent though they are. The magic of this beautiful tropical island is in the sleepy villages, mouth-watering food, rural areas where wild elephants roam, characterful hotels, unexplored regions and delightful local people. The country is diverse, with the historic ports of Colombo and Trincomalee, the remote communities of the North, the ancient Cultural Triangle, the misty inland tea plantations and the laidback beaches of the south and northeast. On this travel blog, we'll share stories from our community of travellers and travel experts to give a little insight into what makes Sri Lanka such a fantastic destination, perfect for families, couples and solo travellers alike..
